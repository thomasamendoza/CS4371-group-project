A Look at Some Popular Breeds

Australian Shepherd

Known for their intelligence, high energy, and striking merle coats, Australian Shepherds are excellent herding dogs and active companions. They thrive with owners who can provide plenty of physical exercise and mental stimulation.

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Instantly recognizable by their short legs, long body, and foxy face, Corgis are affectionate, smart, and surprisingly athletic. They have a big-dog bark in a small package and make loyal family pets.
