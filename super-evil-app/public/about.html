Who are we?

Dog Lover Inc.

We are Dog Lover Incorporated, a proud subsidiary of Evil Pets Incorporated—an organization on a mission to help you love dogs even more. Whether you're a lifelong dog enthusiast or just starting your journey into the wonderful world of wagging tails and floppy ears, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

At Dog Lover Inc., we believe dogs aren’t just pets—they're family. Our passion drives us to create products, content, and experiences that celebrate everything canine. With the quirky spirit of Evil Pets Inc. behind us, we bring a playful edge to our commitment: spreading joy through dog love, one paw at a time.
